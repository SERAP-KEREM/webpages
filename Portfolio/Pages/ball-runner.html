Ball Runner

Technologies Used

Unity, C#, AdMob

Description

This game involves a character running on top of balls and navigating through obstacles. Key features include:

  • Character and Ball System: Implemented a system where the character rises or lowers based on the number of balls they collect or lose.
  • Obstacle System: Designed obstacles of varying difficulty, appearing randomly in front of the player.
  • Endless Levels with Increasing Difficulty: Developed a system for endless levels where the difficulty increases progressively with each level. This system ensures continuous challenge and engagement for players.
  • Gameplay Mechanics: The player must navigate through obstacles on a platform, aiming to reach the finish line with the most coins and balls. Levels increase in difficulty, with endless levels available for continuous play.
  • AdMob Integration: Integrated AdMob to display ads, enhancing revenue generation through in-game advertisements.
